About the Campaign

The Green Earth campaign is a global effort to restore our planet's forests. By planting trees, we reduce carbon dioxide, protect soil from erosion, and provide habitats for wildlife. Our mission is to create a sustainable future through reforestation.

  • Restoring degraded forest lands worldwide.
  • Supporting local communities with green jobs.
  • Enhancing biodiversity with native species.
  • Reducing global warming impact annually.
